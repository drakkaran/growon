How it works

Three simple steps

1

Contribute & Earn

Drop off clean, sorted items at our local hub. Each item is assessed and you earn points based on size, condition, and material.

2

Browse & Claim

Search the catalogue by size, gender, season, and category. When you find something, claim it with your points — no cash involved.

3

Collect & Enjoy

Pick up claimed items at the hub. Our volunteer team ensures every item is clean, labelled, and ready to wear.
